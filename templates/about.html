About This Website


This is a website created for our CS50 final project. We wanted to flip the idea of choosing a concentration on its head; students often focus primarily on or give too much weight to the name of each course. We don't want someone to automatically dismiss a course called 'Engineering Sciences 95r' just because they don't think they're a "STEM person", even though the course is actually about 'Startup Research and Development'! We'd also like to help reverse the trend of picking a concentration, and then trying to find courses to fulfill that concentration instead of first customizing a study plan that consists of exciting courses, and then seeing what concentrations that might lead to.

We have nifty little features like being able to see your suggestions (cumulative across all your test-taking efforts) and selection history, and starring courses to save them for future reference!
